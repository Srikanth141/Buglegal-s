Paying house rent?


To be eligible for HRA exemh4tion.you must meet following criteria:

- You must be living in a rented accommodation
- You must be paying rent to the landlord
- You must be able to provide the rent receipts and/or rent agreement as proof
- If your rent is above Rs 1 lakh per annum, you have to submit your landlord's PAN
You can generate rent receipts here, and submit them as proof. However, they must be signed by your landlord.

For your current rented accommodation, leave the "to month" blank. To enter multiple rents,enter the first, press Continue and come back to this page.

A metro city implies Chennai, Kolkata, Mumbai or Delhi. All other cities in India including Gurgaon, Hyderabad and Bengaluru are non-metros
