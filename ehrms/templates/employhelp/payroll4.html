Can I download documents like payslips, reimbursement & investment proofs, and employee documents in bulk?

Yes, you can download documents like payslips, reimbursements proofs, and investment submission proofs, employee documents in bulk as a ZIP file from your developtreesX Hrms dashboard.

Download Payslips in bulk

Navigate to Reports > Salary Register inside the admin section.

Click on the Export button on the top right of your screen.

Select the date range and employee status (Active/Dismissed Both) for the payslips you need to download and subsequently click on â€œEmail.

Download Reimbursement Proofs in bulk

1. Navigate to Reports > Reimbursements inside the admin section.

2. Follow the same steps as mentioned above for downloading payslips in bulk.

Download Investment Proofs (Tax Deduction proofs) in bulk

1. Navigate to Reports > Tax Deductions inside the admin section.

2. Follow the same steps as mentioned above for downloading payslips in bulk.

Download Employee Documents in bulk

1. Navigate to Reports -> Documents inside the admin section.

2.Follow the same steps as mentioned above for downloading payslips in bulk.
