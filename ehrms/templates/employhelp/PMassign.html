How can the Project Manager create tasks for team members in the HRMS portal?

To assign tasks to team members in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:


You will see the following:

  1. Log into your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In the left-hand menu, click on "Projects".
  4. Click on a project and you see dropdown elements. On the dropdown details page, scroll down to the "Create new task" section.
  5. Click on the "Create New Task" button to create a new task. Fill in the necessary task details, such as Project name and Task Name, and assign a Team lead for that task.
  6. Once you have completed all the necessary fields, click on the "Submit" button at the bottom of the page.
