How can the Project Manager give the status of the project in the HRMS portal?

Give the status of the project in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:

You will see the following:

  1. Log into your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In the left-hand menu, click on "Project Status".
  4. Check your project title and give the status of the project then click on the update status button the updated status of the project can appear in the project status.
