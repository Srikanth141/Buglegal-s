Where can I see the entries made by Hrms in my QuickBooks account?

Hrms creates a list of accounts in your QuickBooks chart of accounts. You can run reports on these accounts to get any specific entry. The main account depicting all transactions in and from your Hrms account is “Hrms Bank a/c”. Run a report on this account to get all the items.
