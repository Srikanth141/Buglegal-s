How can the Project Manager access the projects and tasks in the HRMS portal?

To access your projects and tasks in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:


You will see the following:

  1. Log into your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In the left-hand menu, click on "Projects".
  4. You will now see a list of all your active projects. Click on a project to view its details.

You can manage your active projects by edit option in the dashboard.
