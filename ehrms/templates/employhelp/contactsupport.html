How do I contact support?
  1. If you are on the PRO plan, you can use the chat option to raise a query.
  2. For certain operations-related incidents, you may interact with our email support team.
  3. If you are on the FREE plan, you can make use of our articles and help section and also write to us. Live chat is not supported in this plan.
  4. We do not provide phone support and encourage email or chat usage mostly.
