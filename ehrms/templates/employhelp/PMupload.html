How can the Project Manager upload documents to the HRMS portal?

To upload documents in the HRMS, you can follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In That Dashboard you see Documents section.
  4. In that Documents section, you can upload your documents like payslips, Experience Certificates.
