How do Employees check in/check out and view Attendance?

To upload documents in the HRMS, you can follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. On the left side menu click on the Attendance.
  4. If you want to check in then click on the CheckIn button.
  5. If you want to check out then click on the CheckOut button.
