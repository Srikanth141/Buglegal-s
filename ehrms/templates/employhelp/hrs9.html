How to HR apply for payslip,view past payslip request and apply for advance salary?

Apply for Payslips and Advance salary in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:

You will see the following:

  1. Log into your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In the left-side menu, click on "MY PAY SLIP".
  4. Fill in the form and click on the Request Payslip button.

View Past Payslip Request in the HRMS portal, follow these steps

  1. Click on the View Past Payslip Request button on the left side of the Payslip page
  2. View your past payslips

Apply for Advance Salary in the HRMS portal, follow these steps

  1. Click on the Salary Advance button on the left side of the Payslip page
  2. Apply for Advance Salary
