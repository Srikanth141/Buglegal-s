How to send the meeting link team lead to team member and schedule time with me powered by calender?

To send the meeting link, you can follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In the dashboard project details below send the meeting link option.
  4. click on the send the meeting link > enter the meeting URL and select team memeber then click on the Submit button.
