How do schedule time with me powered by a calendar?

Schedule time with me powered by a calendar in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:

You will see the following:

  1. Log into your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. On the left-hand menu, click on "Projects".
  4. Click on projects > send the Review link > here show the Schedule time with me powered by calendly on the bottom of the page.
