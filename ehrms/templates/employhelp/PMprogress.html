4.How can Project Manager monitor the progress of tasks in the HRMS portal?

To monitor the progress of tasks in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:


You will see the following:

a.Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.

b.Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.

c.In the left-hand menu, click on "Projects."

d. Click on a project and you see dropdown elements. On the dropdown details page, scroll down to the "Employee performance task" section.

f. Click on the "Employee Performance task" button. Here, you will see a list of all the Employees task performance in the project. You can see view its details and check the progress.

h. if want check project status then click on the project status on left side menu bar.
