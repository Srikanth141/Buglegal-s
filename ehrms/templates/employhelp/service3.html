How do I apply for a Leave?

XPayroll supports multiple leave types and employees will be able to select and apply for
them from within their respective logins.


Leaves will be sent to their managers for approvals as it is configured for the employee
under their Basic Profile section. Admins in the account will also be able to approve leave
requests on managers behalf.


Click on Attendance on the left hand side:


You will see the following:


Check-In & Check-Out option to mark your attendance for the day. You can also edit
it later and send it for approval at a day level.


Visibility of leaves assigned to you on the right hand side


An option to apply for bulk leaves or individual leaves by day as you wish


Select the leave type and send it for approval to your manager.


Green Indicates - Present Days


Red Indicates - Approved Leave Days


Brown - Pending Approval from Manager
