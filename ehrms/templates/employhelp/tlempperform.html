How can the Team Leader check the Performance of the Employee in HRMS portal?

You will see the following:

  1. Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. Click on the employee performance task. Here it shows the employee's performance.
