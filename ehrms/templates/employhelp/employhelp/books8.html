I have done the integration. When do I see the entries being made into QuickBooks?

You will see the accounts created and few entries created within 2 hours but we suggest you wait for 24 hours since the syncing of the past data takes up to 24 hours.

Do note that entries are made when the transaction is Successful, hence the entries for the compliance payments may take a couple of weeks to reflect since the compliance payments are made at a monthly cadence.
