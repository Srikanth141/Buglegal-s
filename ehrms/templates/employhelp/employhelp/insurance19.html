What are the prerequisites for an organization to use Insurance?

Your organization has to be a user of RazorpayX Payroll and needs to use XPayroll for employee salary transfers and all applicable compliances like TDS, PF, PT, ESIC.

Your organization has to be on the Pro Plan of XPayroll.

Your organization must have 2 or more full-time employeesStarter plan is available for organizations that have a minimum of 2 employees.Essential and Premium plans are availablefor organizations that have a minimum of 5 employees.
