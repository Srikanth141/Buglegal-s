How do I declare Investments?

XPayroll supports the following:


The ability for employees to select from a Dual Tax Regime


Employees can declare investments and avail of tax exemptions


XPayroll team will ask for proofs in the month of January and verify the documents on
the company's behalf if the setting is enabled (only on the PRO plan)


Post verifications, clarifications, TDS liability is automatically recalculated
on the application


Employees receive Form 16s once the FY is complete and TDS returns are filed
(only on the PRO plan)
