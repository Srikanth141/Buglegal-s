Contractor Payments FAQs

FAQs on the changes in contractor payments

A

written by amisha Dhawan update over a week ago


Q.1.what is the payment structure for contractors? will the customers be charged per transations?

Ans:- effective july 1,2023 Razorpay Hrms customers will be charged for each contractor payment made. For transactions up to INR 25k, customers will be charged INR 5 + GST per payment. For transactions more than INR 25k, customers will be charged INR 10 + GST perpayment FOR example, if you are pay 10,000 to a contractor,please ensure that you have a minimum balance of rs 10,005.90 in your Hrms account.if your balance is 10000,then the payment will not be processed.


Q.2.Is the contractor payment feature only available for customers on the pro plan?

Ans:- the contractor payment feature is now available to all Hrms customers on free and pro plans, and the change in its payment structure applies to all Hrms customers


Q.3.will the new pricing model affect existing customers?

Ans:-yes, the new pricing model for thr contractor payment feature will apply to all Hrms customers, including existing once.starting july 1,2023, customers will be charged rs 5 + GST per payment for contractor transactions up to INR 25K and INR 10 + GST for transactions more than INR 25k.


Q.4.how can i view can track the charges for contractor payments?

Ans:-you will be able to view and track the charges for contractor payments on yoyr Hrms account. after each payment the charges for that payment will be blocked from your balance, and you will receive a consolidates invoice for all payments in tha month at the end of the month


Q.5.how will the new pricing model affect my Hrms costs?

Ans:-The impact of the new pricing model on your overall Hrms cost will depend on the number and frequency of contractor payments made. Bycharging a flat rate per payment, we aim to provide trasparency and ensure that you billed fairly for the service you utlise


Q.6.why is this change being introduced?

Ans:-The change in the pricing model for the contractor payment feature is necessary to ensure that we can continue providing the highest level of services to all your customers. by implimenting a nominal charge per payment, we can maintain improve and quantity of the Hrms service while offering competitive pricing.
