Employee - Get Started

Firstly, welcome to an XPayroll - we are glad you are with us.


As a new employee joining an organization, you would have many questions, but we
hope Payroll is not going to be one of them.


You would have received an email to verify your email - you will be allowed to set up
a password. You can always reset your password by requesting a reset here.


Once you are on the Dashboard, you will be prompted to complete your Profile.


Complete your Profile immediately - this is very important - you will give us important
information including Bank account numbers, PAN, DOB etc which are very critical.


Once completed, you can always edit and make changes to your profile, upload a photo,
set preferences for email etc, by clicking on the top right icon the dashboard:


Few more things to consider:

You will need to declare investments that you plan to make in the year to avail of Tax
Exemptions. You can read more about the process here.


You will be able to access your payslips after your first salary day on the left-hand
navigation panel.


You can follow the instructionshere to raise Reimbursements, and here for Leave
application process.
