How do Employees check Daily Task?

To check Daily Task in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:

You will see the following:

  1. Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. On the left-hand menu, click on the DAILY TASK.
