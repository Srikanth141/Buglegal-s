How can Project Manager communicate with my team members in the HRMS portal?

To communicate with your team members in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:

You will see the following:

a.Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.

b.Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.

c.In the left-hand menu, click on "Projects."

d. In the left-hand menu, click on "send the review link"that is google meet

e. Here, you can communicate with your team lead and team members.
