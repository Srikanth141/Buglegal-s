How to HR add single employee and multiple employees in htms portal?

HR adds the single employee

  1. Log into your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once logged in, you click the people after visible the add one button.

You will see the following:

  1. Click on the add employee button.
  2. Visible the employee onboarding.
  3. After clicking the continue button to display the message successfully added the employee.

How to HR add multiple employees to the HRMS portal?

HR is to add multiple employees through an Excel template

  1. Log into your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you click the people after visible the add multiples.

  1. After downloading the Excel sheet.
  2. We have to add the multiple employees in the Excel sheet.
  3. After uploading the Excel sheet.
