How do the Employees upload documents to the HRMS portal?

To upload documents in the HRMS, you can follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. Click on Documents.
  4. Here upload a new document pick a type of document choose the file write a Description then ofter upload Documents.

In that Documents section, you can upload your documents like payslips, Experience Certificates
