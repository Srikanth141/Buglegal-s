How can HR upload documents in the HRMS portal?

To upload documents in the HRMS, you can follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In That Dashboard you see 'Documents' section.

In that Documents section, you can upload your documents like payslips, Experience Certificates
