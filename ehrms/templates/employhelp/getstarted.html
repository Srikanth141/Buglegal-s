Employee - Get Started

Firstly, welcome to an HRMS - we are glad you are with us.

As a new employee joining an organization, you would have many questions, but we hope HRMS is not going to be one of them.

Once you are on the Dashboard, you will be prompted to complete your Profile.

Complete your Profile immediately - this is very important - you will give us important information including Bank account numbers, PAN, DOB, etc which are very critical.

Once completed, you can always edit and make changes to your profile, upload a photo, set preferences for email etc, by clicking on the top right icon of the dashboard:

Few more things to consider:

You will be able to access your payslips after your first salary day on the left-hand navigation panel You can follow the instructions here to raise Reimbursements, and here for the Leave application process.
