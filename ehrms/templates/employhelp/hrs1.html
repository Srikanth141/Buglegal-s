How can HR view attendance in the Hrms?

If you want to view your attendance, you can follow these steps:




You will see the following:

  1. Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In That Dashboard you see'Attendance' section.
  4. In that Attendance section, you can give attendance by check-in and check-out buttons for that day.
