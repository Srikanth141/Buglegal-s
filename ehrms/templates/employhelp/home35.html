How can the Project Manager Add the Performance of the Employee based on their assigned Task in the HRMS?

Add the Performance of the Employee in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:

You will see the following:

  1. Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. n the left-hand menu, click on "Projects".
  4. Click on Add Employee Performance Task in the Projects.
  5. Fill in the form and then submit.
