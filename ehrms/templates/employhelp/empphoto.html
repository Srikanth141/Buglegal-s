How do Employees upload/update their profile photo?

Upload the profile photo in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:

You will see the following:

  1. Log into your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In the navigation bar, click on "Profile Icon".
  4. In the dropdown of the icon click on the Upload Photo.
  5. Choose your photo and then click on the Upload button.


Update your profile photo in the HRMS portal, follow these steps

  1. In the Navigation bar click on "Profile Icon".
  2. In the dropdown of the icon click on profile.
  3. On the left side you find the Update Photo button click on that button.
  4. Choose your updated photo and then click on the Upload button.
