Free vs Pro Plan

Is the Free plan actually Free?

Yes, it is 100% free. You will be able to invite all your users (unlimited) - both employees and contractors. You will be able to use all the SaaS platform features including directly transferring Salaries to employees' bank accounts. You can also use the Time, Leave and Reimbursement modules that we have along with IT declaration and core HRIS management features.


What is in the PRO plan then?

You get a lot more when you are on the PRO plan. On the PRO plan get automated compliance payments for Salary - stop processing Hrms, and start executing Hrms on the PRO plan.

In the PRO plan, Hrms takes care of all of this for you:

1.PF, PT, ESIC monthly payments & filings

2.PF and ESIC register management, including new UAN generation -video

3.TDS payments monthly and quarterly 24Q filings for salary TDS

4.Year-end tax document verification of employees

5.Form 16 generation at the end of the FY

6.Access to letter generation module

7.Access to group medical insurance via Plum & ICICI Lombard

8.Integrations with Zaggle & Jupiter for flexible benefits & banking needs

9.More integrations with Quickbooks, Slack

10.Live Chat support

How to upgrade to the PRO Plan?

Just drop us a message on the chat or a mail on Hrms@Developtress.com and we'll quickly upgrade you to the PRO plan so that you can sit back and relax while we take care of your Hrms.
