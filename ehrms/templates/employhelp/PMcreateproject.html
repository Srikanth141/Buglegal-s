How can the Project Manager add a new project to the HRMS portal?

To add a new project to the HRMS portal, follow these steps:


You will see the following:

  1. Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In the left-hand menu, click on "Projects".
  4. On the "Projects" page, click on the "Create New Project" button.
  5. Fill in the necessary project details, such as project name, description, Team lead and Team members and project deadline.
