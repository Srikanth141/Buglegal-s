What are the compliance services offered by Hrms?

Hrms helps you with automating all the operational parts of compliances and we are not in the advisory space necessarily.


The following are a part of the offering:

1.PF UAN registration and monthly payments & filings

2.ESI IP registration and monthly payments & filings

3.TDS payments & Quarterly filings (24Q)

4.One-time Tax Document Verification in January

5.Form 16 generation at the end of the year

6.26Q filings if enabled and all contractor payments made via platform


The following are not covered:

1.Company registrations for Compliances like PF, PT, ESIC

2.LWF payments & filings

3.Salary Structuring or HR services

4.PF transfers, withdrawal management etc

5.Assistance with ESI claims

6.Representation services of any kind
