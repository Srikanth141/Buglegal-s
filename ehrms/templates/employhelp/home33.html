How can edit and check the personal information of the project manager in the HRMS portal?

Profile details in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:

You will see the following:

  1. Log into your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In the navigation bar, click on "Icon".
  4. In the dropdown of the icon click on the Profile.



In case you want to change any information go to Edit

Click on the Save Profile button after filling in the form.
