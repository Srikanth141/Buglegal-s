How can the HR check the performance of the Employees in the HRMS portal?

To Check the Performance of the Employee in the HRMS portal, follow these steps:

You will see the following:

  1. Log into your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. In the left-side menu, click on "Employee Performance".
  4. Click on the daily employee task report it shows the daily task performance report of an employee.

Click on the Employee Average Performance it shows the average performance of the employee.


Click on the employee project performance select the particular project name then it shows the performance of every employee.


Click on the employee task performance select the particular task name then it shows the performance of every employee.
