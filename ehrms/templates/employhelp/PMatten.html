How do Project Manager view my attendance in the HRMS?

if you want view your attendance,you can follow these steps:


You will see the following:

a.Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.

b.Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.

c. In That Dashboard you see'Attendance' section

d.In that Attendance section you can give attendance by check-in and check-out buttons for that day

if you want apply for leave by clicking "apply leave" button

How do I view my Payslips in the HRMS?

if you want view your payslips,you can follow these steps:


You will see the following:

a.Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.

b.Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.

c. In That Dashboard you see'MyPaySlips' section

d.In that MyPaySlips section you can view and manage your payslips

if you need advance salary you can apply by clicking 'advance salary' button
