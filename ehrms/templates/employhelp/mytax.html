How can I reduce my tax?

There are three primary ways in which employees can reduce their tax liability -

1. If you pay rent, please ensure that Hrms knows that by putting in the required
information in the tax deduction page. If your rent exceeds Rs. 8,333 per month, then you
also need to provide your landlord's name, address and PAN number.

2. You can make investments, several of which give you tax benefits. For more
information, please click here. If you plan to make some investments in this financial year,
but haven't yet, you can still put in an amount under the correct section so you start
receiving the tax benefits immediately. However, if you do this then you must ensure that
said investments are made before the end of the financial year, or you will get a tax notice
from the government.

3. If you have taken a home loan and are paying interest on it, you can get additional tax
benefits for it. For more information, please clickhere
