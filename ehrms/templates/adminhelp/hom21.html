How Admin can see Advance Salary Requested by Employee?

You will see the following:

  1. Login into Buglegal HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. Go to 'Admin Options' and there you will see 'Advance Salary'.

    4. Click on the 'Advance Salary' and Admin can see the list of employees requested for advance salary.

Once you have clicked the Advance salary admin can see the advance salary requested by the employee.
