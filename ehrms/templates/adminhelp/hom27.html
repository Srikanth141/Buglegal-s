How can Admin delete a document uploaded by an employee?
  1. Login into Buglegal HRMS portal using username and password.
  2. Once you log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. Click on Admin Options > Go to People > select an employee > you have documents section on the right side of employee data.
  4. Click on documents and you have a delete option.
