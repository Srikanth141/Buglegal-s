Hrms Account Setup Guide

Follow these steps to complete your company account onboarding.


Here is a checklist for your HRMS account, please
ensure you go through these 10 steps to get your
account ready for your first payroll.

1. Training Videos

a. For Admins(Get Started Guide)
b. daily live products demo sessions
For employees

2. Ensure you have added all employees:

a. single add: Admin options>people>Add one
b. Add many: Bulk update employee list

3. Let us know what compliances are enabled.

a. Enablecompliances
b. connect compliances portal(if applicable)

4. Update your logo:(this will reflect on payslips)

company details > Logo

5. Set your default salary structure

Admin option>settings>basic info>Default salary structure

6. Check for Missing information

You can track which employee profiles have mandatory information missing.


Admin option>Reports>Missing information

7. Check the Salary Register to reconfirm all salary components.

You can reconfirm if the salary components and the net salaries are accurate here.

Admin option>Reports>SalaryResister
