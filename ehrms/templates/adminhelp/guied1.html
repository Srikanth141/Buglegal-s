HRMS Account Setup Guidance

Follow these steps to complete your company account onboarding.

Here is a checklist for your HRMS account, please ensure you go through these 10 steps to get your account ready for your first HRMS.

1. Training Videos

a. For Admins(Get Started Guide)

b. daily live product demo sessions For employees

2. Ensure you have added all employees:

a. single add: Admin options>people>Add one

b. Add many: Bulk update employee list

3. Let us know what compliances are enabled.

a. Enablecompliances

b. connect compliances portal(if applicable)

4. Update your logo:(this will reflect on payslips)

company details > Logo

5. Set your default salary structure

Admin option>settings>basic info>Default salary structure

6. Check for Missing information

You can track which employee profiles have mandatory information missing.

Admin option>Reports>Missing information

7. Check the Salary Register to reconfirm all salary components.

You can reconfirm if the salary components and the net salaries are accurate here.

Admin option>Reports>SalaryResister

8. Ask employees to update their Tax declarations

The employee would need to Navigate to 'Tax Deductions' so they can update declarations and minimize their taxes deducted.

video walkthrough

9. Add HRMS details as a beneficiary

We assign a unique customer identifier to each HRMS client for transferring funds for executing HRMS.

Adding this unique number to your bank account as a beneficiary is required in advance as most banks have a 24-hour cooling period before funds can be transferred.

you can find your account details by clicking this link(login required)

10. Update the UAN for employees (if PF is applicable)

Admin options>peoples>select any one employee name, Professional Tax & ESI. Do reach us on the live chat in-app, if you need any assistance with this.
