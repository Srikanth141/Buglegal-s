How does the admin add single employee and multiple employees to the HRMS portal?
  1. Admin add the single employee.
  2. Login into Buglegal HRMS portal using your username and password.
  3. Once log in, after you click the 'Admin Options' > Go to People > click on the 'add one' button.

You will see the following:

  1. Click on the add employee button.
  2. Employee Onboarding page will be displayed.
  3. After filling the details and clicking the continue button, it will display the message 'Employee Added Successfully'.



How does Admin add multiple employees to the HRMS portal?
  1. Admin can add multiple employees through an Excel template.
  2. Login into Buglegal HRMS portal using your username and password.
  3. Once log in, you click on People and there 'add multiple' button is visible.

  1. After downloading the Excel sheet.
  2. We have to add the multiple employees in an Excel sheet.
  3. After adding multiple employees to the Excel sheet, upload that Excel sheet.
