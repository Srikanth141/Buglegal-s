Do I get a GST invoice for the insurance payment?

The Insurance offering on HRMS is of the type where developtrees holds the master policy on your behalf.

Due to the nature of this policy, we provide you with a Payment Receipt for the complete premium payment.

This is generated by developtrees for facilitating the transaction with the insurer. Apart from this, we also issue a Certificate of Insurance that contains the Insurance premium and GST details.

These are generated at an employee level and you can use a combination of all of these for your accounting purposes.

Do note that GST input credit cannot be claimed on health insurance premium payments.More information on this is here.
