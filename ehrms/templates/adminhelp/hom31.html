How can admin track their employees time?

You will see the following:

  1. Login into Buglegal HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. Click on Time Tracking > Select a particular employee whose time you want to check Here shows that particular employee's login time logout time and total duration time.
