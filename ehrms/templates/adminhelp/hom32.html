How can admin check all employees documents?

You will see the following:

  1. Log in to your HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. Click on Documents > it shows all employees document data.

OR
  1. Click on admin options > go to reports > Documents > it Shows the employee's documents.
