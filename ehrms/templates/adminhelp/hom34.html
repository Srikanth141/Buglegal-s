How can the admin add custom holidays for the company?

You will see the following:

  1. Login into Buglegal HRMG portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. Click on Admin Options > go to settings > Holidays, Leaves and Attendance > click on Edit button > Add custom Holidays.
  4. Admin can add Custom Holidays by entering both Date and Description and click on the continue button.
