How can Admin check the current status of existing projects?

You will see the following:

  1. Login into Buglegal HRMS portal using your username and password.
  2. Once log in, you will see the main dashboard.
  3. Click on Employee Performance > Project List > Here shows all the existing projects in the organisation.
