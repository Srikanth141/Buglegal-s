How can I make a one-time payment to my employees?

You can now make a one-time payment to your employees outside of their regular Hrms cycle. Examples of a one-time payment can be a performance incentive or other bonuses such as a retention or joining bonus, that you want to pay to an employee immediately.

The payment will be paid out instantly to the employee and will reflect in their upcoming monthâ€™s payslip along with relevant additions to their TDS (in case itâ€™s taxable). When you execute the upcoming month's Hrms, HRMS will exclude the amount that has already been paid via one-time payments from the required Hrms amount.

What this is not:

1. Do not use this for salary arrears - this does not generate payslips. If you have skipped and not processed for the previous month, head to Run Hrms.

2. Do not make compliance payments like PF, TDS, PT, or any other such payments via this module.

3. Do not use this for paying a salary advance. A one-time payment adds the amount paid out to the employee's next gross pay.

TDS on one-time payments

If the payment that you are making is taxable, then HRMS will show you the net payment that will be made to the employee after withholding TDS. The TDS will get paid when the Hrms for that employee is executed.
