How to pay and recover a Salary Advance?

Instant payments during Emergencies

HRMS allows you to pay and recover advance salaries very easily.

Step 1: Go to the left nav bar under Pay Employees > Advance Salary.
Step 2: You can create an advance salary request for your employee under new advance or approve/reject an existing request that an employee has created.
Step 3: Approved requests or Created requests will be added to the pending payments table.

Click on the pay now button on your right and pay them!

Step 4: Enable/Disable advance salary requests from employees from under Settings >HRMS Setup
